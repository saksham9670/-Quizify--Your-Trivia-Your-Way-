## MCQ 1
Question: Which of the following is a defining characteristic of all mammals?
A) Laying eggs
B) Having scales
C) Possessing mammary glands
D) Living in water
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 2
Question:  What is the primary function of a mammal's diaphragm?
A) To aid in digestion
B) To assist in breathing
C) To regulate blood flow
D) To support the skeletal structure
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 3
Question: Which of these animals is a monotreme?
A) Kangaroo
B) Echidna
C) Dolphin
D) Bat
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 4
Question:  Which of the following is NOT a characteristic of most placental mammals?
A) Giving birth to live young
B) Nourishing young with milk
C) Laying eggs
D) Having fur or hair
Correct Answer: C
